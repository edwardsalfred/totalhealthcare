# Total Healthcare Clinic LLC — Brand Extraction

## Brand Snapshot
- **Company:** Total Healthcare Clinic LLC
- **Primary Color:** `#B22A2A` (cardinal/crimson red — from "TOTAL" wordmark and right wing/right caduceus rod)
- **Secondary Color:** `#2A1F5E` (deep navy/eggplant — from "Healthcare Clinic" wordmark and left wing/left caduceus rod)
- **Accent Color:** `#F8F4EC` (warm cream — paper background)
- **Tertiary Tone:** `#0E0A2A` (near-black for high-contrast text)
- **Fonts (current site):** unspecified — site uses generic Weebly defaults
- **Recommended Fonts (rebuild):** "Fraunces" (serif, headings) + "Inter" (sans, body)
- **Tone:** Warm, reassuring, confident, community-rooted
- **Core Message:** "Your health is your wealth — full-spectrum primary care for the whole family in Jackson, MS"

## Logo Analysis
The logo is a strong, classic medical mark:
- Wordmark: "TOTAL" set in a serif (Trajan-style) crimson red, "Healthcare Clinic" in serif eggplant navy below
- Symbol: A two-tone caduceus — left wing & left snake in deep navy, right wing & right snake in red
- The split-color caduceus is the signature visual asset and should drive the entire design language
