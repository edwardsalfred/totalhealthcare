Common Questions

Everything you'd ask before walking in.

Don't see your question? Call (601) 321-9453 — we're happy to help by phone.

Do you take walk-ins, or do I need an appointment?
We welcome walk-ins where the schedule allows, but appointments are always honored first. Calling ahead — even just 30 minutes before — is the fastest way to be seen.
How quickly can I get a DOT physical?
Most DOT physicals are available same-day or next-day. The exam itself takes about 30 minutes, and we issue your medical examiner's certificate at the visit.
Are you accepting new patients?
Yes — we accept new patients of all ages, from newborns to seniors. Most new-patient appointments are confirmed within five business days.
Can I bring my whole family on the same visit?
Yes. Many families schedule back-to-back appointments for parents and children to save time. Let us know when you book and we'll group your appointments.
Do you offer IV hydration as a single session?
Absolutely. Single-session IV hydration is available for rehydration, recovery, or immune-boost. Multi-session packages are also available for ongoing wellness plans.
What if I don't have insurance?
Self-pay patients are always welcome. We provide written cost estimates before any visit so you know exactly what to expect — no surprise bills.
